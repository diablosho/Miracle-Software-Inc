Extra Credit Assignment #1

#1 Create a program that does the following:

Gets the target sum from the user and then determines the first and last integers in EVERY series of positive, consecutive INTEGERS whose sum is the target sum. Output sent to the screen .
For example, if the target sum is 30, your program would output the following:
THE FIRST AND LAST NUMBERS IN EVERY SERIES OF POSITIVE, CONSECUTIVE INTEGERS WHOSE SUM IS 30 ARE:
4...8
6...9
9...11

Turn in your source code and several sample runs.
